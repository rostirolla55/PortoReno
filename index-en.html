Welcome to the Porto district

The Porto district, the beating heart of Bologna, is a crossroads of history, culture, and modernity. Famous for its ancient arcaded streets and proximity to the historic center, it offers an authentic experience of Bolognese life. Explore its main sections: Pugliole, Lastre, and the anecdotes that have shaped its identity.

This is a welcome page. Use the menu to navigate through the 40 detail pages.

The district's history is rich and fascinating, from the artisan workshops of the past to the renovated urban spaces that now host student residences and innovative start-ups. It is a place where the past meets the future.
